# research

Use `kch research` to produce an evidence-focused research plan. Add `--execute` to launch a detached Kiro team.

```bash
kch research "Compare kch and omx runtime gaps" --workers 3
kch research "Compare kch and omx runtime gaps" --workers 3 --execute
```
